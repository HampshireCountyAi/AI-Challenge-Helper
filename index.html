How to Go From Idea to Working App in 2 Hours

🚀 What Is This?

AI App Ideator guides you through a complete app development process using only tools available in Poe. No coding required, no external accounts needed - just natural conversation that leads to working prototypes.

1

Explore Your Idea

Share your problem or vision. Claude helps you think through different angles, validate the concept, and understand user needs through natural conversation.

⏱️ 30-45 minutes
2

Design Your App

Create detailed specifications including features, user experience, and AI integration strategy. Claude guides you through technical architecture and implementation planning.

⏱️ 45-60 minutes
3

Build Your Prototype

App-Creator generates a working web application based on your specifications. Test, iterate, and refine your prototype immediately.

⏱️ 15-30 minutes

🎯 Getting Started

New to app development? Start with "Start New Idea" and describe any problem you've observed in simple terms.
Returning to continue work? Use "Continue Previous Idea" and AI will analyze your conversation to pick up where you left off.
Want to see it in action? Try the test scenarios in "Start New Idea" → Step 2 to see how real problems become working apps.

💡 Pro Tips

💭 Start Simple
Begin with observations like "Teachers spend too much time..." rather than proposed solutions
🔄 Iterate Quickly
Build, test, and refine. Each prototype takes minutes, so experiment freely
🤝 Think Business
Consider who would use this, how they'd find it, and what value it provides
🎯 Stay Focused
One conversation = one app idea. Start fresh chats for new concepts
